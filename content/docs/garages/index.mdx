# MT Garages
Simple but interactive garage system with built in impound system and locations saves

Tired of needing to go to any garage to store your vehicle before server restart or having to go to the impound because your game crashed and your vehicle despawned? This is your best solution.

With MT Garages, your vehicle will always be where you left it and in the condition you left it.
You can also store your vehicle at some garage to prevent it to be stolen when you left the server or if you lost it, you can always recovery it for a little fee.

**Features**
- Simple interactive garage creator for easy configuration;
- Clean minimalistic UI;
- Vehicle presistent locations saved at data base, leave your vehicle and take it where you lefted it the way you lefted it;
- Damage save;
- Mods save;
- Fuel save;
- Built in impound system blocked to certain jobs;
- Fully optimized;
- Bridge system for easy frameworks integrations.

**Dependicies**
- qb-core or qbx_core;
- ox_lib;
- qb-target or ox_target.
