## MT Hunting 2.0
Interactive hunting system with multiple features including XP, tournaments, leaderboard, animals and zones information totally configurable and wrapped in a simple React UI.

# Features
- Multi framework bridge, automatically detects your framework, configured by default for qb, qbx, ox, nd and esx;
- Simple and minimalistic UI made with React and ShadcnUI;
- Hunting shop based on XP;
- Hunting sell shop to sell all the meat and skins you get, also based on the XP;
- Hunting tournaments:
  - Compete with other hunters every week;
  - Win nice prizes;
  - See your progress on the tournament;
  - See the tournaments history and record your wins.
- Hunting leaderboard to show all the hunters progress information;
- Hunting animals species information of ilegal and legal species;
- Hunting zones map with the information of ilegal and legal zones;
- Advanced and interactive stove to cook the meat you hunt;
- Consumeables system to eat the meat you cook;
- Hunting weapons aim blocked for players or person NPCs.

# Dependicies:
- ox_lib;
- ox_inventory;
- qbx_core, ox_core, nd_core, es_extended or qb-core;
