# MT Restaurants V3
Advanced restaurants script for FiveM

Tired of having a lot of resources for each restaurant on your server?
With MT Restaurants you can solve that easily, with a lot of features, creation of recipes ingame and option to configure to any MLO you want with any job you want all with a brand-new in-game creator.

**Features**
- In-game creator menu, easily create any restaurants within a few minutes, unlimited times;
- Management dashboard UI:
    - Revenue and job insights: Keep track of your restaurant progress;
    - Recipes management: Create, edit or delete any recipes, the limit is the creativity;
    - Ingrediets shop: Order ingredients directly from your job society to your restaurant stash;
    - Orders history: Check all the orders created in your restaurant.
- Prepare stations:
    - Create multiple stations for ingredients or recipes prepare;
    - Choose directly from where you want the items to be retrived and delivered.
- POS, Drive-thru & Self-ordering system:
    - Enjoy our amazing ordering workflow;
    - Intuitive UI with all the restaurant's available recipes, easily order them to any customer;
    - Let your customers pay with our receipt UI and ordering stashes system.
- Offline shop system: Let your restaurant employees add items to be sold when there are no employees online;
- NPC Deliveries: Not enought work? No problem, deliver food to NPCs around the map on dead times;
- Ice Machines: Easily transform water and ice to use in your drinks;
- Buffs effects (with integration with our latest HUD script).

**Dependicies**
- ox_inventory;
- ox_target;
- ox_lib;
- oxmysql.
